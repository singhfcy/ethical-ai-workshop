Frequently Asked Questions

International Workshop on The Ethical AI Toolkit for Scholars

Why should I join this workshop?

This workshop offers hands-on insights into 15+ AI tools designed to elevate academic research, writing, and publishing ethically and efficiently.

What ethical issues does the workshop address?

We focus on responsible AI use in academia—avoiding plagiarism, preserving authorship integrity, and understanding biases in AI-generated content.

What benefits do individual participants receive?

Participants gain certificates, guided exposure to AI tools, ethical awareness, and access to a curated toolkit and surprise AI resource.

Who qualifies as a Nodal Coordinator?

Faculty who successfully refer or manage 10+ registered participants will be recognized and awarded a special certificate of appreciation.

Is the workshop open to international participants?

Yes, the workshop is open to scholars, researchers, and students from all disciplines worldwide.

What is the format of the sessions?

The sessions include expert talks, industry insights, and interactive demos of AI tools used in academic research.
