An International Workshop organized by Department of Applied Chemistry,

About the Workshop

This international workshop aims to introduce scholars, researchers, and faculty to a suite of cutting-edge AI tools that enhance scientific research and publishing. The session emphasizes ethical use of AI in literature review, writing, grant drafting, image generation, and presentation while addressing academic integrity, plagiarism concerns, and responsible AI practices.
