International Workshop on

“The Ethical AI Toolkit for Scholars”

6 August 2025 | 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM IST | Hybrid Mode

About the Workshop

This one-day hybrid workshop will train researchers, students, and faculty members in the practical and ethical use of over 15 AI tools across the academic research process.

Topics include: Ethical research writing, literature reviews, AI-generated visuals, compliance with academic standards, and proper citation of AI-assisted content.

Schedule

  • Session 1 (10:00 – 12:00 IST): Lecture on tools + ethical integration
  • Session 2 (12:00 – 1:00 IST): Industry perspectives
  • Session 3 (2:00 – 4:00 IST): Hands-on with AI tools: Trinka, Quillbot, Scispace, etc.
